## Writing Rules

- Keep each release entry short, external-facing, and easy to scan.
- Summarize what actually shipped rather than copying internal working notes.
- If a section has no content yet, write `None` instead of leaving the status ambiguous.
- Call out user impact, operational impact, breaking changes, and follow-up actions clearly when they matter.
